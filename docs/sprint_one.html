Sprint One

Normal engineers fry breadboards, but gantry engineers fry motherboards.

Mechanical

The first iteration of the compound table had a wider footprint and used custom 3D printed sliders as linear bearings on the 8020 frame members. The lead-screw bushings, motor coupers, and a variety of other parts were 3D printed, and all of the flat components were laser-cut.

Electrical

In the very first sprint, we current electrical and controls system for the X-Y gantry on breadboard. It includes:

  • A4988 stepper motor drivers

  • Arduino Uno

  • A 12 Volt Power Supply

  • Peripherals for component configuration and power distribution

Software

During our very first sprint, we started out writing our own gcode parser compatible with A4988 motor controllers. We were able to cover both linear movement solely in the X or Y direction and diagonal movement that requirement X-Y coordination. By the end of the sprint, our gcode parse was able to ultimately achieve arc command and draw a circle.
