Mechanical Overview

It Takes a Mill to Make a Mill.

The mechanical sytem is comprised of the components for holding, moving, and cutting the PCBs as well as the enclosure to keep the user safe. It was designed and fabricated to maximise precision and robustness while making use of scrap and other freely available materials to get as much as possible out of the budget.
